What's Emotional Intelligence? (EQ)

What is it? - The ability to be introstpective, understand your own emotions and self-regulate them. - The ability to understand and manage the emotions of those around you. Five Main Components: 1. Self Awareness: being aware of how you feel and why, and how your actions and re-actions impact those around you. 2. Self Regulation: being able to decide how you will act (and acting in line with your core values and culture) regardless of the emotions that you are feeling. 3. Motivation 4. Empathy 5. Social Skills Who developed it/when? How can we improve it? We can improve our EQ over time.

Why is EQ important?

- A way that top performers differentiate themselves in the workplace - Create overall happiness increase - Build self confidence and in turn makes you less afraid of changes and risks. - Self awareness: you know how and why you're feeling and can maintain balance in life and practice self-care accordingly - A glass half full mentality - Less likely to blame others - Less likely to become overwhelmed and argumentative and have emotional outbursts - Better able to listen to others and make them feel heard and important -- Better overall communication - Highly in-tune with your emotions; able to operate in a more optimal state of less stress
